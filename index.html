ABOUT US


'Sync-X is here to give a contribution to the world'

We here at sync-x, are a crowdsourced initiative, who are working to improve the
status of online education for half of the world's students who are out of schools
education. This problem is far more worse in remote villages in this unprecedented time.

Owing to the challenges faced by schools/colleges, our team has come up with a
software which uses very low bandwidth to stream online content, making it ideal
for places with connectivity issues.We have made an app regarding this purpose
